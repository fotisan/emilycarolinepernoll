FAQ

Short answers, so the first step feels easier.

Do I need to know what I want to work on before we start?

No. Many people arrive with a feeling rather than a clear topic. In our first conversation, we clarify what is happening and what would actually help.

How long are sessions?

Individual sessions are typically 60 minutes.

Do you offer online therapy?

Yes. Sessions are available both in person in Santa Monica and online. We choose the format that best supports your consistency.

Do you take insurance?

I am out of network. If you have out of network benefits, you may be eligible for reimbursement. You can ask during the consultation and we will walk through options.

What is the free 15-minute consultation?

A brief call to see if we are a good fit, clarify what you are looking for, and decide next steps. No pressure and no expectation.
